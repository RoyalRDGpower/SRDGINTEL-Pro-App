The Art of the Visual Prompt

Generative AI for video has evolved rapidly. To get consistent results, you must understand the relationship between camera movement, lighting descriptions, and temporal consistency. Unlike static images, video prompts require a "motion vector" to guide the AI's temporal coherence.

Pro Tech Tip

"Always define the camera lens (e.g., 35mm anamorphic) before describing the subject. This sets the optical physics for the generation engine."

Key Frameworks

  • check_circle Subject Definition: Clear, concise descriptions of the main focal point.
  • check_circle Environment Context: Lighting conditions (Golden hour, HDR, Volumetric).
  • check_circle Motion Directives: Use verbs like 'tracking', 'panning', or 'slow-motion' to dictate flow.
